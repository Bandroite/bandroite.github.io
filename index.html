A little about me

I'm an aspiring UI/UX designer with skills in creating designs that adhere to quality standards and a Quality Assurance Engineer with experience in identifying and resolving bugs in software and web applications through manual testing within an Agile development environment . I am a good communicator both verbally and nonverbally, hard-working, a team player, and able to learn and adapt in a rapidly changing environment.
